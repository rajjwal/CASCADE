What we do

Awareness

Promote awareness, encourage understanding, and increase the acceptance of LGBTQIA students, faculty and staff at Howard University and the families and friends who love them.

Educate

Educate the University community about the history and current status of the gay, lesbian, bisexual, heterosexual (hereafter known as ally), and transgender communities, and to celebrate the diversity

Fundraisers

Using our incredible team's varied interests as our driving force, we host events of a plethora of themes through which we raise funds.
